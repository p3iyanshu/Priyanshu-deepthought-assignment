# Part B — Sourcing & Scaling Strategy

## Objective
To identify ~1000 companies aligned with the target ICP using a structured, repeatable approach.

## ICP Definition
- Manufacturer (not services)
- India-based
- Technical/differentiated product
- Founder-led / engineering-led
- Operating in a growing sector
- Showing active growth signals

## Sourcing Strategy
Used directories, LinkedIn, startup databases, Google search, and industry bodies.

## Filtering Process
Initial pool → remove duplicates → remove services → apply ICP → shortlist.

## Validation Framework
C1–C6 evaluation applied.

## AI Usage & Control
AI used for structuring only. All data manually verified.
